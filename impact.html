Stories of Impact

Food Drive Outside Hospitals

RakShan Foundation serves free food to families of underprivileged patients in the govt. hospitals and also for the children who come from a far-off places to the hospitals for often longer treatments.

Winter Drive For Homeless

Every winter, our partner Uday Foundation runs Blanket Donation Campaign, where they provide high quality warm blankets along with dry ration and dignity kits to the underprivileged living in the open on the streets.

Educating underprivileged children

A success story of Bhawna, whose life was changed by our partner Aarohan when she joined as a student. Now she teaches in the same institution to change the lives of others.

Success Stories

Training centers for women

The waste cloth generated from the cities is channelised to some villages where women are learning stitching on sewing machines at a RakShan training center. Apart from employment and income, this initiative helps the women to become more independent.

Free schooling kits for online education

His father who used to work as a daily wage earner lost his job in the pandemic. Before joining in the RakShan initiative of providing free schooling kits for online education, he along with his family had to go through very hard times due to lack of money to pay the school fees and buy a laptop. But now, after receiving a laptop, he works harder and never misses a class.

It was all possible because of you.
